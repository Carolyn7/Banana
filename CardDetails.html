    Eligibility & Fees

    Please take a moment to review the below eligibility criteria as well as documents required before completing the online application process.

    DBS NUS Alumni Card
    Age 21 years and above
    Income

    S$30,000 and above per annum (Singaporean and PR)

    S$45,000 and above per annum (Foreigner)

    Note: All applicants must be a graduate of National University of Singapore.
    Annual Fees

    S$128.40 (Principal card)

    S$77.04 (each Supplementary card)
    Fee Waiver 2 Years

    Note: All fees indicated are inclusive of GST where applicable.

    How to apply

    DBS NUS Alumni Card

    DBS NUS Alumni Card

    A Credit Card packed with rewards, that gives back to your alma mater each time you spend.

    • Step 1 – Complete the online application, print the form and sign it.
    • Step 2 – Mail or fax the form to us with a copy of your AlumNUS card or commencement scroll and all other required documents (please click here for a document checklist). Click here if you wish to submit your CPF statement online. Extend the suite of DBS benefits and privileges to your loved ones with DBS NUS Alumni Supplementary Cards!*

      • Click here to apply now.

      *To be eligible, applicants must be graduates or staff of the National University of Singapore or its predecessor institutions. Please submit a photocopy of your NRIC (Front and Back) or Passport.

    DBS NUSS Card

    A membership card and credit card in one, which takes care of your NUSS bills while giving you exclusive Platinum privileges, DBS Rewards and dining offers. The essential card for every NUSS member.

    • Step 1 – Sign up for membership at NUSS Guild House.
    • Step 2 – Complete the application form, print the form and sign it.
    • Step 3 – Mail or fax the form to us with the required documents. You may wish to submit your CPF Statement online.

    Alternatively, visit any of the NUSS Guild Houses to get an application form.
